The Art of Coffee Brewing

Every method unlocks a different dimension of Brave Presso's flavour profile. Find your perfect ritual.

Beginner Friendly

French Press

Full-bodied and bold — the French Press preserves all the natural oils in Brave Presso, delivering a rich, intense cup with a velvety texture.

4 minutes 1:15 ratio 92°C

Steps

  1. 1Coarsely grind 20g of Brave Presso beans.
  2. 2Pre-heat your French press with hot water, then discard.
  3. 3Add grounds and pour 300ml water at 92°C.
  4. 4Stir gently, place lid on, and steep for exactly 4 minutes.
  5. 5Press plunger down slowly and serve immediately.

Pro Tip: Don't press the plunger all the way down in one go — do it slowly over 20 seconds to prevent over-extraction and bitterness.

Stovetop Espresso

Moka Pot

Intense, concentrated espresso-style coffee with rich crema. Perfect for making lattes and cappuccinos at home.

5 minutes 1:7 ratio

Steps

  1. 1Fill the bottom chamber with hot water up to the valve.
  2. 2Fine-grind Brave Presso and fill the filter basket evenly.
  3. 3Screw on the top firmly and place on medium-low heat.
  4. 4Remove from heat when you hear a hissing/gurgling sound.
  5. 5Serve immediately for peak flavour.

Use pre-boiled water in the bottom chamber to reduce bitter metallic taste.

Clean & Bright

Pour Over (V60)

Crystal clear with vibrant floral and fruity notes. The pour-over highlights Brave Presso's complex, nuanced flavour profile beautifully.

3 minutes 1:16 ratio 94°C

Steps

  1. 1Rinse paper filter with hot water, then discard.
  2. 2Add 15g medium-fine ground coffee.
  3. 3Pour 30ml water to bloom for 30 seconds.
  4. 4Pour remaining 220ml in slow circular motions.
  5. 5Total brew time: 2:30 – 3:00 mins.

The bloom step (step 3) degasses the coffee and dramatically improves flavour clarity.

Summer Favourite

Cold Brew

Naturally sweet, low-acidity, and incredibly smooth. The slow cold extraction brings out Brave Presso's chocolate and berry notes.

12-15 hours 1:8 ratio

Steps

  1. 1Extra-coarse grind 100g Brave Presso beans.
  2. 2Combine with 800ml cold filtered water in a jar.
  3. 3Stir gently, cover, and refrigerate for 12–15 hours.
  4. 4Strain through a fine mesh or cheesecloth.
  5. 5Serve over ice. Dilute 1:1 with water or milk if needed.

Cold brew concentrate lasts up to 2 weeks in the fridge — make a big batch!

Quick & Versatile

AeroPress

The most versatile brewer. Make anything from espresso-style shots to smooth Americanos in under 2 minutes with zero mess.

2 minutes 1:12 ratio 80°C

Steps

  1. 1Add a paper filter, rinse with hot water.
  2. 2Add 17g medium-fine ground Brave Presso.
  3. 3Pour 200ml water at 80°C. Stir 10 times.
  4. 4Attach cap, flip and press gently for 30 seconds.
  5. 5Stop pressing when you hear a hiss. Serve.

Lower water temperature (80°C) reduces bitterness and creates a sweeter, smoother cup.

Instant Luxury

Instant Coffee (Glass Jar)

Our 24g Glass Jar instant coffee delivers a full Brave Presso experience in under 60 seconds. Perfect for busy mornings.

60 seconds 1-2 tsp

Steps

  1. 1Add 1–2 teaspoons of Brave Presso instant coffee to your cup.
  2. 2Pour 30ml cold water and stir into a smooth paste.
  3. 3Add 150ml hot water or hot milk. Stir well.
  4. 4Add sugar or sweetener to taste.
  5. 5For iced coffee: add ice after mixing. Enjoy!

Mix with cold water first to prevent clumping and get a silky smooth result every time.
