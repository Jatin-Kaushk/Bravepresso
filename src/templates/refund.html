Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Home / Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

1. Return Eligibility

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we accept returns only if:

  • The product was damaged during shipping
  • You received the wrong product
  • The product has a manufacturing defect

Returns must be initiated within 7 days of delivery.

2. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact us via email at hello@bravepresso.com or WhatsApp at +91 98218 46822
  2. Provide your order number and photos of the issue
  3. Our team will review and respond within 24 hours

3. Refund Process

Once your return is approved:

  • Full refund will be processed to the original payment method
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • For COD orders, refund will be via bank transfer (NEFT/UPI)

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after opening the sealed pack
  • Products consumed or used partially
  • Claims made after 7 days of delivery

5. Replacements

In case of a valid complaint, we offer a free replacement instead of a refund (subject to availability). Replacement orders are shipped within 24 hours of approval.

6. Contact

For refund queries: hello@bravepresso.com | +91 98218 46822
