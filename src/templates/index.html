Brew Like a Pro

Coffee Recipes

Master the art of brewing with our curated recipes for the perfect cup.

4 Mins

Classic French Press

A full-bodied, rich coffee that preserves all the natural oils.

  • Coarse grind 20g coffee.
  • Add 300ml hot water (92°C).
  • Steep for 4 minutes.
  • Press slowly and enjoy.
12 Hours

Smooth Cold Brew

Low acidity and naturally sweet, perfect for hot summer days.

  • Extra coarse grind 100g coffee.
  • Add 1L cold filtered water.
  • Steep in fridge for 12-15 hours.
  • Strain and serve over ice.
3 Mins

Pro Pour Over (V60)

Clean and complex cup that highlights the delicate floral notes.

  • Medium-fine grind 15g coffee.
  • Wet the filter paper first.
  • Pour 250ml water in circular motion.
  • Total brew time: 2:30 - 3:00 mins.
