Our Story

Where It All Began

BRAVE PRESSO was born from a simple belief: that every coffee lover deserves access to freshly roasted, single origin beans without paying a fortune. Founded in 2024, we started by partnering directly with smallholder coffee farmers across India's finest growing regions — Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Wayanad.

Our founders spent months visiting plantations, cupping hundreds of samples, and building direct trade relationships that ensure both quality and fair compensation for the hardworking farmers who make it all possible.

The Roasting Process

We roast in small 5kg batches using artisan drum roasters. Our master roaster, with over 15 years of experience, carefully profiles each batch to bring out the unique terroir of our single origin beans. The result? Bold notes of dark chocolate, hazelnut, and wild berries that dance on your palate.

Every bag is roasted on order — we never sell stale beans. When you receive your BRAVE PRESSO, it's guaranteed fresh, with a roast date stamp on every pack.
